About SF Quick Links

SF Quick Links is a Chrome extension designed to enhance your Salesforce experience by providing instant access to essential Salesforce pages. With this extension, users can quickly navigate to important sections of Salesforce with just a right-click.

How to Use

1. Install the Extension

Download and install the SF Quick Links Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store.

2. Enable the Extension

After installation, the extension will be automatically enabled in your browser. Ensure it's active by checking your browser's extension toolbar.

3. Access Quick Links

Right-click anywhere on a Salesforce page and select "SF Quick Link".
