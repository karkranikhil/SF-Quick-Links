Privacy Policy

SF Quick Links Chrome Extension

Back to Home

Effective Date: January 8, 2025

SF Quick Links (the "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Extension. By using the Extension, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension does not collect any personal information. It operates entirely within the confines of your browser and Salesforce pages, and does not send any user data to external servers.

2. Permissions

To function properly, the Extension requires the following permissions:

  • Context Menus: To create and manage the right-click context menu for quick access to Salesforce pages.
  • Tabs: To read and modify the current browser tab's URL for redirection purposes.

Host Permissions: The Extension operates on Salesforce-related domains only, specifically:

  • https://*.salesforce.com/*
  • https://*.salesforce-setup.com/*
  • https://*.force.com/*
  • https://*.cloudforce.com/*
  • https://*.visualforce.com/*

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. The Extension only modifies the current browser tab's URL to redirect you to the Salesforce pages you select from the context menu.

4. Data Security

Since the Extension does not collect any personal information, there are no specific data security measures needed to protect your data. However, we are committed to keeping the Extension free of security vulnerabilities and ensuring it operates safely within your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not share or interact with any third-party services. It operates entirely within your browser and interacts only with Salesforce pages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
